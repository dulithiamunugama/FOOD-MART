Emerald Valley Farms Cooperative

Emerald Valley Farms Cooperative in central Sri Lanka, a community of small-scale farmers, uses traditional methods to produce fresh, flavorful vegetables. Foodmart's partnership supports rural livelihoods and preserves countryside beauty.

Sunrise Agro Hub

Sunrise Agro Hub, a Sri Lankan organic farming facility, provides premium-quality vegetables year-round. Foodmart collaborates with them to ensure sustainable agriculture and environmental stewardship in the region.

Golden Harvest Collectiv

Golden Harvest Collective, a cooperative of coastal farmers in eastern Sri Lanka, produces nutrient-rich vegetables in sandy soils. Foodmart partners with them to provide customers with fresh, locally sourced vegetables from the golden shores.
