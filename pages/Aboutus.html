About us

Kandy

155 2/1 2nd Flr Prince Street,
Kandy.
Sri Lanka

Situated in the heart of Kandy, at Prince Street you'll find the Central Market branch bustling with activity. This spot is well known for its array of fruits and veggies sourced from local farms. The bakery here whips up batches of bread, pastries and cakes while the grocery section stocks a mix of international goodies to cater to everyones tastes. Don't miss out on the top notch services at the butchery and seafood counters for a shopping experience.

Colombo

121 A New Moor Street,
Colombo.
Sri Lanka

Over on 121 A New Moor Street, near the University of Colombo sits another branch that caters to students and locals alike. With its layout and modern amenities this spot is popular for its grocery selection along with a well stocked deli and an organic products section. Customers rave about the quality and freshness at the butchery and seafood departments.

Anuradhapura

3rd Flr Paul VI Centre 24 Malwatte Road,
Anuradhapura.
Sri Lanka

In the surroundings of Anuradhapura at 303 Lakeside Drive lies another branch offering a peaceful shopping retreat away from city lifes hustle. This Lakeside Drive location is famous for its sourced produce along, with a bakery serving up freshly baked treats daily.
